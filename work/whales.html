WHALES UNHOOKED
A non-profit organization that aims to protect whales and brings awareness to the downside effects of whaling.


OBJECTIVE

Whaling has been a subject to intense debate over the years. As someone who feels strongly for animals and their rights, I knew this was a subject I wanted to touch upon. Along with designers Ashley Pae and Vivian Lee, we decided to come together and create a campaign for this cause.

PROCESS
Research In order to have a better understanding of campaigns worked, we each looked up two different campaigns to see what tactics were used. In my case I looked Dove's Realy Beauty Campaign targeted mostly towards women, and Amnesty International's Campaign on spreading awareness for child soldiers. While these cases were pretty different from our topic, it helped us recognize how these cases were successful in their own right.





Branding After much researching, we began by giving our campaign an identity. To start off, we sketched out our ideas and looked at one another's ideas and talked about what we liked. By the end of our discussion we were able to choose the qualities we wanted in our logo and put them all together.
