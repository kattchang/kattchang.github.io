UI/UX EXERCISES


As my interest in UI/UX design grew, I wanted to do some practice to gain skills in this field of design. This prompt me to do a quick search on Google leading me to the Daily UI website – an email subscription that sends out a daily User Interface design prompt. For every exercise I would give myself a time constraint of 40 minutes to 1 hour allowing me to plan out each design more efficiently.
