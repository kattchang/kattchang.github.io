BOW WOW
A brand focusing on bringing healthy and natural treats for dogs of all sizes.


OBJECTIVE

The goal for this project was to develop a branding and packaging solution for a fictional company selling rawhide twists treats for dogs. Through the designs, the company aims to represent the dog's playful personality and connect with the customers on a personal level.

PROCESS



Branding Through my sketches and exploration, I knew that I wanted a playful name and logo mimicking the dog's happy personality. Two logos were made for different purposes in which the secondary logo (right) is used on promotional goods, billboards, etc. The color was decided through my love for Disney's Pluto who, to me, embodies the epitome for playfulness.

Packaging
I wanted the packaging to represent the company to its fullest. Watching how my dogs moved around my place, I saw how they were very eager to jump at me to express their joy. This movement resembled springs which I decided to incorporate in my design. Before moving on to the final design, I tested out different ways of incorporating the springs using mockups. In the end, the springs were implemented in the design interior to have a subtle, yet surprising, effect.

FINAL THOUGHTS
Throughout this project, I ran into plenty packaging problems. I needed to be meticulous and precise with my craft in order to have everything in place. Although the trial and error process was tedious, it was a refreshing experience to hone my skills.
