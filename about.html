ABOUT
A designer who loves all dog breeds.

I’m an interdisciplinary visual designer who loves helping companies, brands build a voice and connect with their audience through good design. Good design isn’t only about making things beautiful—it has to work.

I use a human-centered design approach to problem solving, sharing my designs to targeted audiences to better understand their needs.

When I’m not designing you can find me scrolling through instagram of animal celebrities, lounging around with my two dogs, or playing games on my Nintendo Switch.

  • Totoro is my favorite film from Studio Ghibli.
  • I have trypophobia—an aversion to irregular patterns or clusters of small holes, or bumps.
  • I love matcha, add a splash of almond milk and it’s the definition of perfection.
